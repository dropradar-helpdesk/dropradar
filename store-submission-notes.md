# DropRadar Store Submission Notes

Updated: 2026-07-07

This is the working copy for App Store / Google Play review text. It is not legal advice.

## App Positioning

DropRadar is an unofficial information organizer for hobby drops, limited goods, collaborations, manga/light-novel releases, and event planning. It links users to official pages and keeps summaries short and original.

Do not describe DropRadar as affiliated with Pokemon, Bandai, Shueisha, Square Enix, Hololive, Yoshinoya, McDonald's, or any other rights holder, publisher, manufacturer, restaurant chain, shop, or event organizer.

## App Review Notes Draft

DropRadar is an unofficial organizer for official-announcement links. It does not rehost copyrighted product images, official logos, manga/anime images, videos, music, or long excerpts. Current location is optional and is requested only after the user taps the location button; it is used temporarily for nearby sorting and is not stored by DropRadar. User requests are moderated before any public listing is adopted. The app may show official search/source links, and users are instructed to confirm official pages before purchase, lottery entry, or travel.

No purchase, lottery success, stock, route success, or schedule accuracy is guaranteed.

## Privacy Label Draft

- Data used to track users: No.
- Data linked to the user: Not for the public browsing flow.
- Location: Optional approximate/current location for nearby sorting only; not stored by DropRadar.
- User content: Tracking request terms and votes may be stored for duplicate prevention and admin review.
- Identifiers: Anonymous duplicate-prevention fingerprints may be used for loginless request/vote abuse control; raw fingerprints are not public.
- Purchases: None at current release.
- Advertising data: None at current release.
- Diagnostics/analytics: None beyond platform/server operational logs at current release.

## Store Screenshots

- Use only the DropRadar UI, neutral icons, and text listings.
- Do not include official product images, logos, manga/anime panels, PV screenshots, or fake character-like images.
- Show the non-affiliation and official-link-first nature clearly.
